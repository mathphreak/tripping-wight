Creates a flash.media.Sound by different audio decoders

show the text "Click to browse from your local hard drive"

if we click:
	stop listening for a click
	
	load the file to play

once the file loads:
	stop waiting for it to load
	tell the user it loaded
	
	load the WAV or AIFF sound depending on file extension

	tell the user the length of the sound
	
	tell Flash to play the sound
